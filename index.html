The Common Area and Exercise
Since our astronauts will be in an isolated hab, cooperation and collaboration between each astronaut is key. This common area will is an open area with a variety of activities to keep the astronauts entertained and healthy, such as workout machines and interactive games. The open area also features a couch and a dining table, which doubles as a conference zone for the 6 astronauts.
The Lab
The lab will feature a variety of cabinets to hold different equipment: fume hoods, chemical disposal bins, radioactive safety zones, etc. It's on the first floor of the hab to ensure that samples are transported as quickly and cleanly as possible to be analyzed and dissected.
The Clinic
The clinic is a state-of-the-art facility. It hosts emergency medical equipment and relief stations. There are two resting beds that fold into the walls, vertical shelves for medications and supplies, and a station that collects data to monitor the mental and physical health of the astronauts.
Living quarters
Each person will have their own personalized living quarters. There is vertical shelving for storage and a bed that folds into a work desk. The privacy of each astronaut is paramount; this is a place sacred for alone time.
Bathrooms
There will be a total of two bathrooms, one located on the first floor, and a second one on the second floor, which also has a compact shower.
Kitchen
The kitchen is a smaller area that has basic kitchen supplies such as a microwave, a counter that holds supplies in lower cabinets, and foldable chairs.
